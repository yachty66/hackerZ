hackerZ
What
hackerZ is a hackathon where people come together, get a problem and start with finding a solution for the problem. We work together with companies so that the problem is company related and could be everything - also not computer related problems.
How
The problem gets introduced and through clever matchmaking you get to know your group of other problem solvers. You have then 24 hours till our jury will vote which of the groups hitted the solution for the problem the best. In these 24 hours you get everything provided what you need - food, drinks and sleep places.
Why
The hackathon is a great opportunity to meet other like minded people. Because of our group building algorithms you can be sure to get a great match - perhaps you find a group with which you can build your next unicorn.
